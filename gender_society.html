Summary of Key Laws

RA 9710 – Magna Carta of Women

Guarantees equal rights for Filipino women in all areas.

RA 6725 – Anti-Discrimination

Prohibits gender-based discrimination in employment.

RA 7877 – Anti-Sexual Harassment

Protects individuals from harassment in workplaces and schools.

RA 9262 – Anti-VAWC

Protects women and children from violence and abuse.

SOGIE Bill

Proposed law to prevent discrimination based on gender identity.
